Getting Started!

You spawn in the world with nothing.
Find a stone next to a rock.
Use the stone to mine a tree and collect wood.
Craft and place a workbench.
Craft sticks.
Craft a stone pickaxe, stone axe and a stone sword.
Mine rocks to get a total of ten stones and craft and place a furnace.
For now you can fuel the furnace with wood.
